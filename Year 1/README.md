## 📖 Overview

Year 1 provided a strong foundation in computer science, combining technical knowledge, analytical thinking, and personal development. Throughout the year, I was introduced to fundamental topics such as programming, digital logic, discrete mathematics, computer architecture, system analysis and design, human-computer interaction, probability and statistics, and information systems.

Through various assignments, laboratory activities, projects, presentations, and group discussions, I gained practical experience in programming using C++, problem-solving techniques, system modelling, user-centred design, statistical analysis, and low-level computer operations. These experiences strengthened my logical thinking, analytical skills, technical competency, and ability to apply theoretical concepts to real-world situations.

In addition to technical courses, subjects such as Integrity and Anti-Corruption as well as Appreciation of Ethics and Civilizations enhanced my understanding of ethics, social responsibility, cultural diversity, and professional values. These courses helped develop a balanced perspective on the role of technology within society.

Overall, Year 1 established the fundamental knowledge and skills required for more advanced studies in software engineering, data engineering, system development, and computing-related disciplines. It also strengthened my communication, teamwork, critical thinking, and lifelong learning abilities, which continue to support my academic and professional growth.
