# 🎯 Summary of What I Have Gained from This Course

The Appreciation of Ethics and Civilizations course provided valuable understanding of ethics, civilization, cultural diversity, and social harmony from various societal perspectives. Throughout the semester, I learned about the importance of ethical values, national unity, intercultural understanding, and responsible citizenship in building a harmonious and progressive society.

The course explored contemporary issues related to economics, politics, culture, social development, and the environment through ethical and moral perspectives. These discussions enhanced my awareness of the importance of professional ethics, social responsibility, and mutual respect within a multicultural community.

### Key Learning Activities

#### 📝 Poetry (Puisi)

The poetry activity encouraged creativity and reflection on ethical values, culture, and social issues while improving communication and self-expression skills.

#### 🎮 Game Proposal

The game proposal project required creative thinking and teamwork to develop an educational concept that promotes ethical awareness and civic values through interactive learning.

#### 🧠 Mind Map Activity

The mind map activity helped organize and visualize important concepts related to ethics, civilizations, cultural diversity, and national unity, improving understanding and critical thinking.

#### 📚 Examination

The examination assessed understanding of ethical concepts, civilization studies, contemporary issues, and the application of ethical principles in daily life and society.

### Key Learning Outcomes

- Ethics and moral values
- Cultural and civilization studies
- National unity and social harmony
- Critical thinking and analysis
- Communication and presentation skills
- Teamwork and collaboration
- Social responsibility
- Intercultural understanding

## 🌟 Personal Reflection

One of the most valuable aspects of this course was learning how ethical values and cultural understanding contribute to harmony within a diverse society. The discussions and activities encouraged me to think critically about contemporary issues and evaluate them from different ethical and cultural perspectives.

The group activities, presentations, and creative projects strengthened my communication, teamwork, and problem-solving skills while increasing my appreciation for cultural diversity and mutual respect. These experiences helped me better understand the importance of social responsibility and ethical behaviour in both personal and professional life.

Overall, this course successfully developed my ethical awareness, civic-minded thinking, and interpersonal skills while encouraging a deeper appreciation of different cultures and civilizations.
