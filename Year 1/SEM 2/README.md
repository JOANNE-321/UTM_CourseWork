## 📖 Overview

During Semester 2, I was exposed to a combination of technical, analytical, and soft-skill development courses that further strengthened my foundation in Computer Science. The semester covered important areas such as object-oriented programming, computer architecture, human-computer interaction, system analysis and design, probability and statistics, as well as ethics and civilization studies.

Through assignments, laboratory exercises, projects, presentations, and group activities, I gained practical experience in software development, system modelling, user experience design, statistical analysis, low-level programming, and project planning. These learning experiences enhanced my programming abilities, analytical thinking, problem-solving skills, and understanding of how technology solutions are designed and implemented.

In addition, courses related to ethics and civilization helped strengthen my awareness of social responsibility, cultural diversity, and professional values. Overall, Semester 2 provided a balanced learning experience that developed both technical competencies and interpersonal skills, preparing me for more advanced computing courses and industry-related projects in future semesters.
