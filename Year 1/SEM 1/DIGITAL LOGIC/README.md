# 🔌 Digital Logic


# 🎯 Summary of What I Have Gained from This Course

The Digital Logic course provided a strong foundation in digital electronics and computer hardware systems. Throughout the course, I learned that digital electronics forms the basis of many modern technologies, including computers, microprocessors, robotics, automotive systems, and industrial control systems.

The course introduced fundamental concepts of digital systems, logic gates, Boolean algebra, combinational and sequential circuits, and programmable logic devices. These topics helped me understand how digital components work together to process and store information within electronic systems.

Through laboratory experiments and practical activities, I gained hands-on experience using simulator software, digital circuits, and electronic devices. These practical sessions reinforced the theoretical concepts learned in class and improved my ability to analyze, design, and troubleshoot digital circuits.

## 🌟 Personal Reflection

One of the most valuable aspects of this course was the opportunity to combine theoretical knowledge with practical experience. Working with digital circuits and simulation tools allowed me to visualize how electronic systems operate and strengthened my understanding of computer hardware fundamentals.

The course also improved my logical thinking, analytical skills, and problem-solving abilities through circuit design and troubleshooting activities. These experiences helped me appreciate the importance of digital logic as a foundation for more advanced subjects in computer science and engineering.

Overall, this course successfully enhanced my understanding of digital systems and provided essential knowledge that supports future learning in computing, electronics, and system development.
