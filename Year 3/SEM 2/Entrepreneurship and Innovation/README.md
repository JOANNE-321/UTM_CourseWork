# 💼 Entrepreneurship and Innovation

# 🎯 A. Summary of What I Have Gained from This Course

This course introduced me to the concept of entrepreneurship and how businesses can create positive social impact. Through the **Sandwich Sanctuary** project, my team sold sandwiches within the UTM community and used the proceeds to support a charity program at **Bright To Special Home**.

The project allowed me to experience the process of planning, promoting, and managing a small business. I learned how to work with team members, communicate with customers, and use social media as a marketing platform. Besides business skills, I also gained a better understanding of social entrepreneurship and how businesses can contribute to society.

One of the most meaningful experiences was visiting Bright To Special Home and interacting with individuals with Down syndrome and autism. This activity helped me develop empathy, social awareness, and a greater appreciation for community service.


# 🌟 B. Personal Reflection

The Sandwich Sanctuary project was the highlight of this course. It showed me that entrepreneurship is not only about making profit but also about creating value for the community.

Through this project, I improved my teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills. The experience of selling sandwiches and later interacting with individuals at Bright To Special Home was both meaningful and rewarding.

Overall, this course helped me understand the importance of combining business knowledge with social responsibility. It has encouraged me to become more socially aware and to use my skills to contribute positively to society in the future.

## 🚀 Conclusion

Overall, Entrepreneurship and Innovation was a valuable course that provided practical experience in entrepreneurship and community engagement. The knowledge and experiences gained from the Sandwich Sanctuary project will be beneficial for my future personal and professional development.
