# 🎯 A. Summary of What I Have Gained from This Course

## Overview

Professional Communication Skills 2 provided valuable exposure to professional communication techniques, particularly in proposal writing, pitching, negotiation, and formal group discussions. Throughout the course, I developed the ability to communicate ideas clearly, organize arguments effectively, and present information confidently in professional settings.

## 🤝 Negotiation Activity

The negotiation activity provided practical experience in planning and conducting professional negotiations. Through role-play discussions and negotiation exercises, I learned how to communicate effectively, understand different perspectives, and work towards mutually beneficial outcomes.

### Key Learning Outcomes

* Negotiation strategies and techniques
* Active listening skills
* Conflict resolution
* Persuasive communication
* Decision-making in group discussions
* Professional interaction and teamwork

### Key Takeaway

This activity taught me the importance of preparation, communication, and compromise in achieving successful negotiation outcomes. It also improved my confidence in handling discussions and resolving disagreements professionally.

# 🌟 C. Personal Reflection

This course significantly improved my confidence in professional communication. Before taking this course, I often felt nervous when speaking in front of an audience or presenting ideas formally. Through various activities, especially the negotiation exercises and pitching presentation, I became more comfortable expressing my thoughts and communicating with others.

The course helped me understand that effective communication is not only about speaking clearly but also about listening actively, organizing ideas logically, and understanding the needs of different audiences. The negotiation activity taught me how to handle discussions professionally and work towards mutually beneficial solutions, while the pitching activity strengthened my ability to present ideas persuasively.

In addition, group activities improved my teamwork, interpersonal communication, and adaptability by encouraging collaboration and problem-solving in different situations. These experiences helped me become a more confident and effective communicator.

Overall, this course enhanced my professional speaking, presentation, negotiation, and interpersonal communication skills, which will be valuable in both academic and workplace environments.

## 🚀 Conclusion

Overall, Professional Communication Skills 2 was a valuable course that strengthened my communication, presentation, negotiation, and interpersonal skills. The knowledge and experience gained from this course will help me communicate more effectively and confidently in future academic, professional, and workplace settings.
