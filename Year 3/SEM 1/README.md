Year 3 Semester 1 provided a balance between technical knowledge and professional skill development. The courses strengthened my understanding of Artificial Intelligence, Database Systems, Enterprise Systems, and Measurement Techniques while also improving my communication, presentation, and language skills.

Through projects such as **SimuLearn (Artificial Intelligence)**, **SQL and MongoDB Database Systems (Database Programming)**, and **UTHM Academic Advising System (Enterprise Systems Design and Modelling)**, I gained practical experience in solving real-world problems through system design and technology solutions.

Overall, this semester enhanced both my technical competency and professional development, preparing me for more advanced topics in Data Engineering and future industry projects.
