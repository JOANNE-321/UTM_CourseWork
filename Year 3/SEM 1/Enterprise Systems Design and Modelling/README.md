# 🎯 A. Summary of What I Have Gained from This Course

## Overview

During the Enterprise Systems Design and Modelling course, I gained valuable knowledge of enterprise information systems, enterprise architecture, and business process integration. The course provided exposure to Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems, enterprise architecture frameworks such as Zachman and TOGAF, system integration, data governance, customer relationship management (CRM), and implementation strategies used in modern organizations.

Through project-based learning and industry-related activities with PPG, I gained practical experience in analyzing real-world enterprise problems, gathering system requirements, designing enterprise architectures, and proposing suitable enterprise solutions. These experiences helped me understand how enterprise systems support organizational operations and strategic decision-making.

In addition, the course strengthened my teamwork, communication, analytical thinking, and problem-solving skills through collaborative projects, presentations, and industry engagements. Hands-on modelling activities and discussions on real enterprise scenarios further improved my understanding of how enterprise systems enable digital transformation and improve business performance.

Overall, this course enhanced both my theoretical knowledge and practical skills in enterprise systems design, modelling, and business process improvement.
